# Roadmap

Board Game Library's edge over play-trackers (BG Stats) and catalogers (BG Catalog) is that it's an actual **library** — lending + multi-collection — that you **self-host** across platforms. This roadmap leans into those two differentiators while closing gaps with the competition.

Status legend: ✅ done · 🔜 next · 💡 idea

## Lending (the "library" core)
- ✅ Check-out / check-in to members, due dates, overdue flags, loan history
- 🔜 Overdue **reminders / notifications** (mobile push, web/email)
- 🔜 Per-member **borrowing limits** and a "currently out" view per member
- 💡 **Reservations / holds** and a waitlist when a game is out
- 💡 **QR / barcode** check-out (scan a game and a member to lend in one step)
- 💡 Lending analytics — most-borrowed games, member activity, turnaround time

## Multi-collection
- ✅ Collections per BGG username, tabs, and compare (shared / unique / A-not-B) — desktop
- 🔜 **Parity**: bring multi-collection to the **web** and **mobile** apps
- 💡 "**Who can lend me X?**" — cross-collection availability lookup
- 💡 Union / merged view across selected collections, with per-collection ownership badges
- 💡 Comparison **stats & charts** (overlap %, collection value, unique counts)

## Play tracking & stats (close the gap with BG Stats)
- ✅ Play log + win leaderboard; winner picked from listed players
- 💡 Scoring **categories / rounds**, cooperative & team results
- 💡 Per game-and-player **charts**, H-index, win-rate trends
- 💡 Optional play **timer** and game-specific scoring templates

## Collection management (ideas from BG Catalog)
- 💡 Ownership **statuses** beyond own/favorite: wishlist, want-to-buy, pre-ordered
- 💡 **QR share** a game so a friend can add it to their list
- 💡 Shareable "victory" / collection images

## Platform & data
- ✅ Self-hosted desktop + web + mobile, offline, backup export/import
- 🔜 Keep **feature parity** across desktop / web / mobile as features land
- 💡 One-tap LAN discovery / pairing between the desktop host and mobile clients

> Priorities are rough — the lending and multi-collection items are the intended focus, since that's what no competitor offers.
